### **Summary of "The Match" (How the Math Interlocks)**

- **The Chip Equation:**
    
    - SoftCom needs **40 chips** for "Hardware Blitz."
        
    - Silicore's "Split Fab" provides **50 chips** to SoftCom. -> **MATCH.**
        
    - _Result:_ SoftCom can only succeed if Silicore agrees to Option 2.
        
- **The Enterprise Equation:**
    
    - PearCom needs access to business clients for "Enterprise Pivot."
        
    - CorpSolutions "System Integrator" provides that access. -> **MATCH.**
        
    - _Result:_ PearCom cannot roll the die for Option 2 unless CorpSolutions signs the deal.
        
- **The AI Equation:**
    
    - PearCom needs AI for "Enterprise Pivot" (otherwise their product is dumb).
        
    - They can buy from SoftCom ("Arms Dealer") OR OpenAIco ("Open Model"). -> **MATCH.**
        
    - _Result:_ PearCom has two sellers to negotiate with, creating a bidding war.
